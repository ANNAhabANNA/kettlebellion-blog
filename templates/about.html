Who Are We?

KettleBellion connects you with like-minded people who love to snatch, clean and jerk kettlebells. Our friendly community is here to help you make kettlebell training a part of your fitness routine.

Our Story

In March 2020, KettleBellion was founded by a group of kettlebell enthusiasts who met each other in the local crossfit club and became passionate about healthy lifestyle. During the tough times of pandemic the online concept of sharing their knowledge of kettlebell training gained the appreciative audience and has formed the present community.
